# Chapter Title

## Files

- `README.md`: Overview, outcomes, prerequisites, and completion checklist
- `Theory.md`: Core teaching material
- `Exercises.md`: Practice tasks
- `Labs.md`: Guided implementation work
- `Solutions.md`: Reference answers and explanations
- `Interview.md`: Interview questions and answer outlines
- `References.md`: Reading list

## Quality Bar

A chapter is complete when:

- The learning objectives are explicit.
- The theory explains concepts before abstractions.
- The labs produce runnable code or inspectable artifacts.
- The exercises include recall, application, and design questions.
- The solutions explain reasoning, not only final answers.
- The interview section includes beginner, intermediate, and senior-level prompts.
- References point to primary docs or durable technical sources.
