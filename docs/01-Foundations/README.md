# 01 - The AI Engineering Landscape

## Subtitle

Understanding the AI revolution, LLMs, AI agents, and why software engineering is changing.

## Chapter Promise

This is not a "What is AI?" chapter. You can get that from any intro video.

This chapter gives you the map you need before learning tools, frameworks, agent loops, MCP, RAG, memory, and evaluation. The goal is to help you see the full AI engineering landscape as a system.

## Estimated Study Time

2 to 3 hours

## Difficulty

Beginner to intermediate

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you should be able to answer:

- Why did ChatGPT change the software industry when earlier AI products did not?
- Why are companies moving from chatbots to AI agents?
- What is the difference between AI, ML, deep learning, LLMs, copilots, and agents?
- What does an AI engineer actually build?
- Where do MCP, agent loops, harnesses, LangGraph, CrewAI, OpenAI Agents, and tool calling fit?
- Why is the LLM only one part of a production AI system?
- How does an AI coding tool such as Cursor or GitHub Copilot work at a conceptual level?
